ROBOTICS

During my freshman year, I was intimidated by the high school. Being the youngest in the school, I was hesitant to do a lot of extracurriculars, especially clubs. However, one day, my friend asked me to join Robotics with her. I denied her twice before finally deciding to give in. I knew absolutely nothing about robotics and didn't know what I could contribute to team. Little did I know that I would find myself in a friendly and innovative place where I was able to experience how it felt to be on a team. On the team, I not only got to witness the "behind the scenes" of building a robot, but was also able to make some great friends from different grades and different countries.
