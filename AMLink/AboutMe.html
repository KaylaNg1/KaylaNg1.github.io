About Me

Hi! My name is Kayla Ng and I'm just a high school junior who likes to code sometimes...

Growing up, I've always been very diverse in my hobbies. From sports to music, to learning ASL, I love trying new things and am always looking for new opportunities to further my knowledge. My most recent interest was the world of computer science, beginning my freshman year of high school. To be honest, I didn’t know a lot about computer science. The first thing that would pop into my mind when thinking of computer science was programming and programming from my point of view seemed extremely complex and difficult. However, during my freshman year, I decided to take a computer science elective offered by the high school and found myself enjoying coding. For the past 3 years, I have continued to take all of the computer science classes offered to me and now have a basic-moderate understanding of various programming languages: Visual Basic, Java, Python, C#, HTML. I don’t have much experience, however, I’m continuing to challenge myself in order to advance my knowledge of computer science and hope to in the future, pursue a career in the field.
